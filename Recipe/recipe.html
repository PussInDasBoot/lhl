Vegetable fried rice recipe

Ingredients list

  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup broccoli, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Directions
